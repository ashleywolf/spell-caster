# 🔮 Spell Caster

Tower defense by shouting spell names. Yell "FIREBALL" to throw fire, "SHIELD" to block, "LIGHTNING" for chain damage. Unrecognized words cast random chaos spells. The speech recognition mishears are half the fun.

## How to Play

Open in Chrome or Edge and allow microphone access. Enemies approach your tower — shout spell names to fight them off. There are 5 waves with a boss at the end. Say anything not in the spell book for a random chaos effect.

## Spells

- **FIREBALL** — throw fire at enemies
- **SHIELD** — block incoming attacks
- **LIGHTNING** — chain damage across enemies
- **FREEZE** — slow enemies down
- **HEAL** — restore your tower's health
- **Anything else** — chaos spell (random effect, good luck)

## Tech

- Web Speech API (Chrome/Edge only)
- HTML5 Canvas
- Single file, no build step

## Input

Voice (speech recognition, continuous + interimResults)
